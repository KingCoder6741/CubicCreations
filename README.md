# 📦 Cubic Creations | Corporate Profile & Studio Manifesto

Welcome to **Cubic Creations**. We are a premier design and additive manufacturing studio bridging the gap between digital imagination and physical reality. This document serves as our foundational blueprint—defining who we are, what we build, and how we operate.

---

## 🗺️ Our Core Mission

> *"To transform voxels, pixels, and ideas into tangible artifacts of creativity."*

At Cubic Creations, we believe that great characters, brilliant engineering concepts, and tabletop worlds shouldn't be trapped behind a computer screen. We empower artists, collectors, brands, and developers by providing master-tier 3D modeling and high-precision fabrication services.

---

## 🛠️ What We Do (Our Capabilities)

Our studio operates across three core domains:

1. **Digital Sculpting & 3D Asset Design:** 
   Translating 2D concept art, sketches, or text briefs into high-poly, print-ready, structural 3D models using industry-standard CAD and digital sculpting software.
   
2. **Precision Fabrication (The Lab):**
   Utilizing state-of-the-art SLA (Stereolithography) resin and FFF (Fused Filament Fabrication) technologies to manufacture figures with micro-textured surfaces and seamless structural integrity.

3. **Artisanal Post-Processing & Painting:**
   Transforming raw plastic and resin prints into retail-ready premium collectibles through meticulous sanding, priming, and hand-painted airbrushing.

---

## 🎯 Our Target Markets

We build bespoke physical solutions for:
* **Tabletop Gamers & RPG Creators:** Custom 28mm–75mm scale miniatures, terrain, and dice vaults.
* **Indie Game Developers:** Bringing digital video game protagonists to life for limited-edition physical merchandise and promotional events.
* **Collectors & Pop-Culture Enthusiasts:** High-end, one-of-a-kind display statues.
* **Prototyping & Industrial Design:** Physical validation for product concepts and parts.

---

## 📐 Our Core Values (How We Work)

* **Precision Down to the Micron:** If it isn't crisp, it doesn't leave the shop. We reject flawed prints and prioritize clean support-removal.
* **Respect the Lore:** When building a client’s custom character, the details matter. We listen closely to backstories to capture the right pose, expression, and texture.
* **Material Innovation:** We constantly test new engineering resins, flexible filaments, and pigments to push the boundary of what a 3D figure can endure and look like.
* **Eco-Conscious Workflow:** We actively recycle our filament scrap, filter our wash waste, and use bio-friendly PLA plastics wherever structurally viable.

## 👥 Studio Structure

* 🎨 **Design Department:** Conceptual artists, character sculptors, and CAD engineers.
* 🖨️ **Print Operations (The Lab):** Hardware technicians managing print queues, slicing software, and machine maintenance.
* 🖌️ **Finishing Studio:** Master painters, assembly technicians, and quality control experts.
